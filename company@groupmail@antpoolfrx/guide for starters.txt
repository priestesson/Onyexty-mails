How to become a member and deposit with us

1. Sign up/log in on our website @ https://btcforex.uk
2. Click on Deposit funds 
3. Select the plan you want to invest in from the list of plans.
4. Input the amount of deposit in the Amount column provided
5. Scroll down, select the means of deposit (perfect money,bitcoin) 
6. Click on spend (this will automatically direct you to the payment page) 
7. Copy the company's wallet address, and proceed to make your payments. 

8.Click on save after making payment,your deposit will automatically reflect on your dashboard.
